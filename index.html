PUC SITE

A PUC Site is a platform or service where vehicle owners can obtain a Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate, verifying that their vehicle meets the prescribed emission standards. These sites are authorized by the government and equipped with devices to measure vehicle emissions. The PUC certificate is mandatory to ensure environmental compliance and helps reduce air pollution caused by vehicles.

CHANDRAYAN SITE

The Chandrayaan site refers to the lunar exploration missions led by ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation). It showcases the achievements and data from the Chandrayaan missions, aimed at studying the Moon's surface, mineral composition, and exosphere. These missions mark India's significant contributions to space exploration.
